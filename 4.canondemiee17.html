Canon demi ee17 (a.k.a 조삼카)

Description

With styling way ahead of its time, Canon’s top of the line half-frame camera from 1966 is a stunner.
It is even smaller than the second generation QLs but still easy to handle and fully manual.
It has a very bright viewfinder, fast f1.7 lens, and shutter speeds from 1/500 - 1/8.
