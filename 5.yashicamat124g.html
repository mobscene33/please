Yashica Mat 124G (A.K.A 조빅카)

The Yashicamat is a family of MF (medium format) photo cameras.
The last of the family is the most famous: it's the 124G.

The Yashicamat is a TLR (twin lens reflex) which means that it has two lenses,
a lower lens that actually takes the picture and an upper lens that is only used for viewing and composing.
TLRs have many advantages, including low weight and very quiet operation.
Since there is no moving mirror, hand-held images are often sharper than if an SLR (single lens reflex) was used.
