Minolta 7SII (a.k.a 조치투)

Description

The Minolta Hi-matic 7SII is a great compact rangefinder that was introduced in 1977, and was the last high specification fixed lens rangefinder made by Minolta.
It features a 40mm f/1.7 lens comprising 6 elements in 4 groups that is very sharp, and has excellent contrast.
The 40mm focal length is very convenient for street photography, and the very compact size of the camera means that it can be carried anywhere with ease, and used with discretion.
